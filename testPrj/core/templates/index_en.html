Brief Description

"Manas" is not just an epic but the greatest work of oral folk art passed down from generation to generation. It tells a story of courage, honor, and eternal traditions of the Kyrgyz people. Immerse yourself in the world of heroic deeds and spiritual heritage!

Why read the epic "Manas"?
  1. Millennia-old history passed through generations
  2. Profound philosophy reflecting the values of the people
  3. Unique poetic form and the grandeur of oral creativity
  4. Included in UNESCO's list of Intangible Cultural Heritage
Why must we protect the epic "Manas"?

The epic "Manas" inspires new generations, fostering bravery, justice, respect for elders, and love for the homeland. Protecting it is not only about preserving cultural heritage but also about caring for the nation's future, its traditions, and moral values. By safeguarding the epic, we strengthen our roots and fulfill our duty to ancestors, contemporaries, and descendants.

Spreading the epic "Manas" abroad

The epic "Manas" is a unique heritage of Kyrgyzstan, recognized as a UNESCO masterpiece. Its dissemination abroad plays an important role in popularizing Kyrgyz culture. Through cooperation with international organizations and translations of the epic into other languages, "Manas" becomes accessible for study and admiration worldwide.
